# Saving Results / Outputs

## When results are saved

At the end of a quiz run, QuizMD asks whether you want to save answers locally.

## Where outputs go

- Quiz attempts: `answers/<quiz-name>-<attempt>/`
- Files written:
  - `answers.json`
  - `answers.txt`
- Room boxing transcripts:
  - `answers/room-sessions/`

## What exports are good for

- `answers.json`: automation, analytics, and scripting.
- `answers.txt`: quick review and student feedback sharing.

## Privacy note

Saved outputs can include student answer text. Store and share these files according to your classroom or organization policy.
